Seeds Feeds and Needs is a radical organic food co-operative located within Edmonton Alberta. Started as an Occupy Edmonton working group in 2012, we now grow food in gardens all throughout the city and beyond. We are a non-hierarchical organization that works under the premise of mutual aid with the belief that access to food is a right.

We are currently looking for garden space, tools, and volunteers.
If you are able to help, please join our Facebook group for meeting time information.
Alternatively, you can contact us at seedsfeedsandneeds@outlook.com
Food will be available to order toward the end of the season.
